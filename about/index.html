About Us

Whether you're here for your morning pick-me-up or a cozy afternoon retreat, we serve rich coffee, fresh pastries, and a warm atmosphere.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Conestoga Coffee Shop is a local gem nestled in the heart of the community. Our mission is to create a warm and inviting space where friends and family can gather over a cup of coffee.

Our Values

We believe in quality, sustainability, and community. We source our beans from local roasters and prioritize eco-friendly practices in everything we do.
